BMI Calculator

Understanding BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a numerical value of a person's weight in relation to their height. It is a simple and widely used method for categorizing individuals into different weight groups, allowing for a quick assessment of their overall health status.

Why is BMI Important?

BMI serves as a valuable tool to assess whether an individual is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese. It can help identify potential health risks associated with weight-related conditions, such as heart disease, diabetes, and hypertension.

BMI Categories

The BMI categories are as follows:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal Weight: BMI 18.5 to 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 to 29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater

Interpreting Your BMI

Once you've calculated your BMI using the calculator above, refer to the categories to determine your weight status. It's important to keep in mind that while BMI is a useful screening tool, it may not consider individual factors like muscle mass, bone density, and body composition. Consult a healthcare professional for a more comprehensive assessment of your health.
